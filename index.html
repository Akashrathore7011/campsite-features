Campsite Features

Take a tour through the features of Compsite platform and see what it can do for you.

Custom media and scheduling features are a part of the Pro plan.

A feature rich free account

Our free account is second to none, offering features for free that you'll have to pay for with other services.

  • Complete control of your Campsite branding, including colors and fonts
  • Change the title of your Campsite and add an intro
  • See how many times your links have been clicked
  • Add images to your links from your Instagram account
  • Add your social accounts to your Campsite
  • Pin links to the top of your link list
  • Archive and restore links

Embed your Campsite profile on your website

Take the power of Campsite and embed it directly on your website. We make it easy with only a few steps.

Design with Canva

Canva is a powerful online design tool that now is directly integrated into Campsite. With it you can design images that can be attached right to your links!

Campsite Pro

Upgrade to our Pro plan and unlock powerful features.

Schedule your links to go live with other content

Set a start or end time for each of your links to control when they should appear on your Campsite profile.

View a day-by-day breakdown of link clicks

Wondering how active your link has been? With our link stats you can get a view of either 7 or 28 days, which help you make important decisions based on actual data.

Setup a custom domain and control your profile metadata

Have a domain that you've purchased or looking to get one? It can be set up with your Campsite profile so you have a custom URL that is right for your audience.

Select a title, description, and image to show up when you share with Facebook, Linkedin, and other social sites. We even provide a preview for how it will look when you share to help you create the best metadata.

Three Campsites in one Pro account

Possibly our most powerful feature, the ability to have multiple Campsite profiles without having to log in and out to access each. We make it easy to switch between and manage each one.

Upload your own images or select from thousands of images with Unsplash

Looking for more media options than just Instagram? We've got you covered with custom media uploads and Unsplash

Collect emails from your social media followers

Grow your email list with our email signup link. It can integrate directly with MailChimp or Google sheets and have up to five merge fields.
